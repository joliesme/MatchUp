A Better Fit

We develop a list of what job seekers and employers are looking for. Since everyone's profile for their perfect job is different, with a more comprehensive list of factors, we are able to give a better fit for both job seekers and employers.

Focused Screening

Companies save time and money screening through unrelated CVs while students can focus on industries and roles that suit them.

Quality Assessments

We suggest to students reasons why application may fail and what they are lacking. For companies, just like a head-hunting firm, we recommend a list of the most suitable interns based on a holistic assessment of the candidate.
