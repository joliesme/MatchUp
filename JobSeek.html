How Does MatchUp work

Profiling of students would be based on soft skils (eg. their career aspirations, personality, character, goals, and interests) and hard skills (eg. CV and past experiernces). Our specially designed algorithm will be used to match companies and students together.

Upon signing up, students will go through a series of questions and psychometric tests to create their own unique MatchUp Profile. This profile will be used to match them to jobs that are currently hosted on our platform.
