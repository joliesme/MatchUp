MatchUp (matchup.sg) is a platform that matches talent with companies. MatchUp utilises intelligent job matching algorithm to help people find a job they love. Through MatchUp, job seekers and employers have transparency into competing opportunities and job details. We make the recruiting process quicker and more efficient. MatchUp was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Singapore.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the MatchUp alogrithm work?

Profiling of students would be based on soft skils (eg. their career aspirations, personality, character, goals, and interests) and hard skills (eg. CV and past experiences). Our specially designed algorithm will be used to match companies and students together.

Upon creating a job listing, employers will be given flexibility to input specific traits and/or characteristics, as well as skills sets and experiences that will most suit their needs.

With our specially created algorithm, MatchUp will curate a list of student profiles that suit best to your given criterion.
