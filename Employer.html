Why Use MatchUp

1. Recommendation of the top and most suitable candidates

2. Pre-screening for you - save time and money on looking for suitable interns, focus on hiring senior managment/retaining talents

3. Customizable screening criteria. Input the specific traits/characteristics you are looking for and we will help you devise screening questions to identify the right candidate!

How Does MatchUp work

Profiling of students would be based on soft skils (eg. their career aspirations, personality, character, goals, and interests) and hard skills (eg. CV and past experiernces). Our specially designed algorithm will be used to match companies and students together.

Upon creating a job listing, employers will be given flexibility to input specific traits and/or characterisitcs, as well as skills sets and experiences that will most suit their needs.

With our specially created algorithm, MatchUp will curate a list of student profiles that suit best to your given criterion.
